# Mainnav

This component is the primary means that your users will use to navigate through your portal. It includes horizontal navigation and branding to identify your site.

## Fluid

By default, the Mainnav content is contained within a max-width container. Use the `fluid` prop to remove this constraint and allow the content to stretch to the full screen width.

- Use `fluid` mainnav when you need the navigation bar to span the full viewport width.
