# Footer

The footer item component organizes links under a clear, descriptive title within the footer. It helps users easily navigate to related resources or information, ensuring clarity and accessibility.<br/>The footer contains supporting information for your service at the bottom of your website. All .gov.sg digital services shall contain a Global Footer Bar across all pages. The Global Footer Bar should include the name of the digital service, contact information, a privacy statement and the terms of use.
