## Active state 

Both SgdsSidenavItem and SgdsSidenavLink have `active` state. 

As a menu type, SgdsSidenavItem will be automatically assigned `active` to true when one of its SgdsSidenavLink children is active. When this happens, SgdsSidenavItem menu will be open on first load

As a link type, add `active` prop to SgdsSidenavItem to achieve the active styles
