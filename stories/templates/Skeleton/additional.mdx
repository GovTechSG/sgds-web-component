## Rows

To skeletonised paragraph text, set a number to `rows` prop. 
As the gap between the rows are fixed to `--sgds-gap-xs`, the rows will auto-sized (appear thicker or thinner) depending on the `height` of the skeleton.

  <Story of={SkeletonStories.Rows} />
</Canvas>
