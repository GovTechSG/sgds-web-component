# Blog / Content Page Template

Success story or article page with breadcrumb navigation, title + metadata header, rich body content with sections and stats, and call-to-action. Ideal for news, case studies, impact reports, and long-form content.

## When to use

- Blog posts, news articles, or announcements
- Success stories or case studies
- Impact reports or achievements
- Long-form content pages with structured metadata
- Articles that showcase results with stats and sections

## Block anatomy

```
Blog / Content Page
├── Masthead + MainNav (Application Shell)
├── Header section (breadcrumb, overline, h1, description)
├── Introduction section (body copy)
├── Challenge section (heading + list)
├── Solution section (multiple subsections with h3)
├── Results section (accent background + stats grid)
├── Next steps/conclusion section
├── CTA button
└── Footer (Application Shell)
```

## Customisation notes

- **Breadcrumb navigation**: Update breadcrumb items to match your site hierarchy
- **Article title & overline**: Update the category tag (e.g., "Success Story") and main headline
- **Subtitle**: Modify the description below the title
- **Introduction**: Replace with your opening paragraphs
- **Challenge section**: Update the heading and list items to describe your actual challenges/problems
- **Solution section**: Add, remove, or modify the subsections (h3 headings) to match your solution components
- **Results section**: Update the 3 statistics with your actual metrics and descriptions
- **Conclusion section**: Replace the "What's Next" content with your conclusion or next steps
- **Metadata**: Update publication date and author/organization name
- **CTA button**: Customize button text and link (e.g., "Read More Stories", "Learn More", etc.)
